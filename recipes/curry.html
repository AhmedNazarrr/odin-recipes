Indian Chicken Curry (Murgh Kari)

Description

This is a simple and delicious Indian chicken curry made with a blend of spices. Serve with rice or naan for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the chicken breasts with salt and set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute the onions until browned. Remove the onions and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, saute the chicken pieces until browned. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, and garlic. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Return the chicken and onions to the skillet. Add the yogurt, tomato paste, coconut milk, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper. Stir together.
  6. Cover and simmer over low heat for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
