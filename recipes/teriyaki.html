Oven Baked Chicken Teriyaki

Description

This is an easy oven-baked chicken teriyaki recipe made with a simple marinade. Serve over rice for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 skinless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine cornstarch, cold water, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger, and black pepper in a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer over low heat and cook, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens and bubbles.
  3. Arrange chicken thighs in a baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
